## O1C-0019 decision

O1C-0019 is a packetized incremental living reader, not another sweep of the same
controller.

1. Train and deploy the same quantity: compare cumulative replace, legacy
   re-integration and a learned incremental delta head; never add a cumulative
   query as if it were new evidence.
2. Bind critic credits to the exact frozen reader SHA. After reader training,
   replay bounded BUILD memories with that final reader and refit the critic from
   zero on stationary marginal NLL rewards.
3. Present H64/H65/H96 for one key coordinate as one multiresolution packet with
   raw odd/common fields and mixed Möbius differences. O1 learns the useful
   orientation; no hand-authored scalar decides it.
4. Score every affordable state-addressed candidate or a target-conditioned cheap
   preview stream. Remove the hash-32 bottleneck.
5. Replace compulsory minimum coverage with soft coverage/age attention plus a
   finite starvation trigger. Strong coordinates may be revisited immediately;
   no coordinate can disappear forever.
6. Add learned `HOLD/STOP/DECAY` and per-horizon carrier slots. Initial 256-bit
   scouting remains complete, but later evidence is accepted, shrunk or rejected
   by learned marginal utility.

The existing H96 proof record already contains H64 and H65 prefixes. A full
three-horizon packet over all 256 bits therefore costs at most
`2 * 96 * 256 = 49,152` physical conflicts, below W3 `57,600`, rather than the
legacy separately billed `115,200` logical conflicts.

O1-O will compose the isolated/direct, packet/raw, packet-plus-Möbius,
packet-plus-gate and packet-plus-gate-plus-stationary-critic variants on the six
already opened pools. No new sealed target is spent until leave-one-BUILD-out
results are positive and stable enough to justify a new disjoint gate.
